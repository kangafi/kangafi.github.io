KANGA

K A N G A - is uniting assets, protocols and chains to provide the most efficient use of capital, best value swaps and maximum yield. We do this by combining token management of all assets into mobs which sits on top of efficient use case specific bonding curves for trading, yield stacking combining multiple protocols, yield strategies that automatically provision capital to receive the highest returns and cross-chain jump capabilities which abstract away the complexities of the underlying blockchains and simplifies the user experience.
