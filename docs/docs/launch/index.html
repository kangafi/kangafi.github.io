Launch

DRAFT VERSION ONLY#

This is a draft version and will be modified.

Goal#

The goal of the adoption campaign is $1000 a day in Token Holder Rewards. This involves the following key metrics

  • Pools
  • Liquidity Providers
  • Traders
  • Token Holders

Pools#

  • KANGA-ONE
  • KANGA-xKANGA
  • KANGA-ETH
  • KANGA-BSC
  • KANGA-MATIC
  • KANGA-AVALANCHE

Liquidity Providers#

Liquidity Gauges will be used for the Adoption Campaign

  • Duration - 30 days
  • Kanga Tokens - 1,000,000 (0.1% of Total Supply)
  • Distributed Evenly Over all Pools

Traders#

An air drop of 1,000,000 (0.1% of Total Supply) will be distribued to all traders on the Kanga Platform within the first 30 days. This will be distributed based on the proportion of the total Trading volume.

Token Holders#

Adoption Fund will double token Holders rewards when the $30 Million Trading Volume has been met.

Note: The Kanga Team and Foundation will not earn rewards until their tokens have been vested. Token Holders and Public Sale will be eligible to earn rewards on their unvested tokens immediately However those rewards will be acummulated and released at the time of vesting

Mechanisms#

  • Adoption - Airdrop
  • Adoption - LP Gauges
  • Token Holder Rewards
  • Token Holder Vesting
  • Liquidity Provider Rewards
  • Multichain Rewards
